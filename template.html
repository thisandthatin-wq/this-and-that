About

Hi, I'm the person behind This & That.

I watch a truly unreasonable amount of reality TV so you can decide what's actually worth your time. No breathless hype, no paid-for praise. Just an honest read on what's brilliant, what's a mess, and what's both at once.

This & That started as late-night group-chat rants about who deserved to win. Now it's a proper review page: a score, a verdict, and the story behind why a show hooks us. Movies are up next.

Come argue with me in the comments. That's half the fun.
